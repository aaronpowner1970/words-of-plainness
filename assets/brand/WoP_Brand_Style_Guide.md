## Brand Overview

Words of Plainness is a ministry dedicated to sharing the restored gospel of Jesus Christ in accessible, welcoming language. The visual identity draws from a **library aesthetic**â€”evoking warmth, wisdom, scholarship, and timeless truth. The design should feel like entering a sacred study: inviting, contemplative, and reverent without being cold or institutional.

**Core Brand Attributes:**
- Warm & Welcoming
- Scholarly yet Accessible
- Christ-Centered
- Timeless & Classic
- Inviting to All Faith Backgrounds

## Imagery

### Photography Style

- **Warm, muted tones** â€” avoid harsh whites or saturated colors
- **Library, study, contemplative settings** â€” books, warm lighting, wood textures
- **Nature with spiritual overtones** â€” light through trees, sunrise/sunset, peaceful landscapes
- **Avoid:** Stock photo clichÃ©s, overly modern/corporate imagery

### Background Images

The primary background image is a library scene. When using imagery:
- Always apply a brown overlay for text readability
- Maintain the warm, scholarly atmosphere
- Images should evoke contemplation, not action

### Infographic Style

Based on the Chapter 1 infographic:
- **Soft gradient backgrounds** â€” light cream/blue at top fading to warmer tones
- **Simple iconography** â€” line icons or minimal illustrations
- **Hierarchical layout** â€” pyramid, steps, or vertical flow showing progression
- **Citation references** â€” small bracketed numbers `[cite: 12]`
- **Text boxes** with subtle backgrounds and rounded corners
- **Symbolic imagery** â€” cross, light rays, hands, books, temple elements

## Slideshow Presentations

**Title Slide:**

## Writing Tone & Voice

### Brand Voice Characteristics

| Do | Don't |
|----|-------|
| Warm and inviting | Cold or institutional |
| Scholarly but accessible | Academic jargon |
| Confident yet humble | Defensive or argumentative |
| Personal ("we," "our") | Distant or impersonal |
| Christ-centered | Denomination-focused |
| Inviting exploration | Demanding conversion |

### Key Phrases

- "Where Seekers and Saints Find Common Ground"
- "A Christ-Centered Ministry"
- "Words of Plainness"
- "Hunters and gatherers of knowledge"
- "Come and see"
- "Add light to what you already have"

### Scripture Style

- Use the full book name on first reference (e.g., "1 Corinthians 12:3")
- Abbreviate in subsequent references (e.g., "v. 4")
- Scripture quotes in italic Crimson Pro
- Citations in Gold, smaller size, following the quote

## Logo & Wordmark

### Primary Wordmark

**WORDS OF PLAINNESS**
- Font: Crimson Pro, 600 weight
- Letter-spacing: 0.05em
- Color: Gold `#C4943A` or Cream `#F5F0E6` depending on background

**A Christ-Centered Ministry**
- Font: Source Sans Pro, 400 weight
- Color: Cream @ 80% opacity
- Smaller, positioned below main title

### Usage Guidelines

- Maintain clear space around wordmark (minimum height of the "W")
- On dark backgrounds: Gold or Cream wordmark
- On light backgrounds: Deep Brown `#1A1510` wordmark
- Never stretch, distort, or add effects to the wordmark
