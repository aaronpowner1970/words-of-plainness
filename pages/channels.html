VR Chat Ministry

One of the most unique aspects of this ministry—interfaith discussions in virtual reality. People from around the world, from every background imaginable, gather to discuss faith, doubt, scripture, and life.

What Happens

Open discussions where seekers can ask hard questions, believers can share their perspectives, and everyone practices respectful dialogue across differences.

Who Comes

Atheists, agnostics, Catholics, Protestants, Latter-day Saints, members of other faiths, and everything in between. The only requirement is respect.

How to Join

Sessions are announced through the newsletter. You'll need VRChat (free, available on PC and VR headsets). No VR headset required—desktop mode works fine.

Schedule

Sessions vary based on travel schedule. Subscribe to the newsletter or check back here for upcoming dates and times.
