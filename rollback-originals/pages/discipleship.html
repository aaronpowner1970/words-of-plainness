Tools for Daily Faith

Discipleship Apps

Discipleship is faith in action—not earning salvation through works, but expressing love through living. These tools are designed to support your daily walk with Christ, helping you engage intentionally with His teachings and covenants.

Featured App

Keep My Commandments

"If ye love me, keep my commandments." This daily discipleship tool helps you track your intentional efforts to live covenant principles—not as a checklist for earning grace, but as a way to notice where your heart is reaching toward Christ.

How It Works

Each day, reflect on specific covenant commitments and note your efforts and growth. Over time, patterns emerge that reveal both your progress and areas where the Spirit might be inviting deeper change.

Features

Daily reflection prompts, personal journaling space, progress visualization, scripture connections for each principle, and optional accountability sharing.

A Note on These Tools

These apps are aids, not replacements for personal scripture study, prayer, and the guidance of the Holy Spirit. Technology can support discipleship, but the real work happens in your heart and in your daily choices.
